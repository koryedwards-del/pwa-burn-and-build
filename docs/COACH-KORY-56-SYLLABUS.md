# Coach Kory — 56-Day Syllabus (done for you)

**Source:** *Eat to Lose Fat* (Kory Edwards, rev. 2024) — book flow adapted for daily app coaching.  
**Method:** One Koryism (premise) + one rep per day. You lead; they conclude. No lectures.  
**Universal frame:** *Athletes eat on purpose. You're on an 8-week prep. Win today.*

**How to use this:** Copy each row to Trello/Notion. Rearrange if you want. This is the heavy lifting — you only tweak what feels wrong.

## Koryism bank (extra lines — drop in anytime)

- Eat random food, get random results. Eat specific food, get specific results.
- If you don't wanna look like your mom, don't do what your mom does.
- Success leaves clues.
- Burn & Build is a comment on what we see working all around us.
- Hunger is a basic life function — like sleeping and going to the bathroom.
- You can't make up for what you missed, but you can get back on track.
- Most people eat the same food every day anyway — choose food that helps you look good and lose fat.
- Exercise works the body. Eating properly reduces body fat.
- No book can give your personal formula — that's what the Burn Engine is for.
