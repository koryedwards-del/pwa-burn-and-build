Privacy Policy

Burn & Build is a web-based personalized diet program. This policy describes what we collect and how we use it.

What We Collect

When you create your custom diet on our website, we collect:

  • Email address
  • Preferred first name
  • Sex
  • Height, age, and current weight
  • Body fat percentage and test method
  • Body composition test date (optional)
  • Job type, workday stress, and lifestyle details
  • Exercise type and hours
  • Wake time
  • Newsletter preference (optional opt-in)

We use this information to calculate and save your diet.

When you use the menu planner, your meal selections and related program data are stored with your program on our servers when you access the site with your email.

Where Your Data Lives

On our servers. When you complete the questionnaire, we save your email, your answers, your calculated diet, and menu planner state so you can open your program after purchase. We also store whether your account has paid access to Burn & Build.

In your browser. Session and local storage may hold your email and program handoff data so pages load without signing in on every visit. Clearing browser data may require you to enter your email again.

Payment processing. Checkout is handled by Stripe. We do not receive or store your full card number. Stripe provides us with payment confirmation and the email used at checkout.

Service Providers

We use trusted providers to run Burn & Build:

  • Stripe — secure checkout and payment processing
  • Render — hosting for the plan creator and API that saves your program
  • GitHub Pages — hosting for our marketing site and web program

These providers process data only as needed to deliver the service. We do not sell your personal information.

What We Don't Do

  • We do not sell your personal information
  • We do not display third-party advertising
  • We do not use third-party analytics or ad trackers on the site
  • We do not store your credit or debit card details

Purchases

Burn & Build is a one-time purchase (currently $149) through Stripe Checkout on the web. Stripe processes the payment; we receive confirmation so we can unlock your program report and menu planner.

Email & Newsletter

Your email is used to save your plan, verify purchase, and unlock your program. If you opt in to the newsletter during onboarding, we send a weekly motivator you might find beneficial. If not, unsubscribe anytime.

Children's Privacy

Burn & Build is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Your Choices

You can email us to ask about your data or request deletion of your saved plan and contact record. Clearing browser data on your device removes local session data stored there.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, we will post the revised version on this page with an updated date.
